Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs with grated parmesan.
  4. Toss pasta off the heat with pancetta, then the egg mixture. Season.
