Spaghetti Carbonara

Classic Italian comfort — silky, salty, simple.

  • ⏱️ 15 min
  • 🍽️ Serves 2–3
  • 🧑‍🍳 Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in well-salted boiling water.
  2. Fry pancetta until the edges are crisp.
  3. Whisk eggs with grated parmesan.
  4. Toss pasta off the heat with pancetta, then the egg mixture. Season.
