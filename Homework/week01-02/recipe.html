Classic Tomato Pasta

Serves 2 · Prep 10 min · Cook 20 min

Ingredients Steps Tips

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil, chopped

Steps

  1. Boil salted water; cook pasta until al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil over low heat (30–60s).
  3. Add tomatoes; simmer 10–12 min. Season to taste.
  4. Toss with pasta. Finish with basil.

Tips & Variations

  • Add chili flakes for heat.
  • Top with grated parmesan.
  • Use gluten-free pasta if needed.
